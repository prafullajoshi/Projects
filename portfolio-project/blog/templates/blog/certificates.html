    Seminars & Certificates

    MultiOS Installation

    I have attened MultiOS Installation seminar conducted by Dr Vijay Gokhale sir in 2017. In this seminar Gokhale sir have taught me all basic fundamentals of operating system in detail. He had chosen total 9 Os to install on his own single machine, out of them three OS wer to be installed with virtual machine and other were installed natively. He also had installed MacOS X on the system which is not MacOS Compatible i.e., normal PC. Such type of OS is known as hackintosh. This seminar was conducted for two days, In this seminar, sir showed us demo of his own machine having multiple operating systems, he also showed Mac OS without having Mac Book. This seminar had impacted on me to the great extent. I had also tried installing multiple operating system on my laptop. Many things that we prented to be known but we actually don't know them at all. Such things I learned a lot in this seminar.

    Vishwapreneur Program

    I had attened Vishwapreneur Program which was related to entrepreneurship and development that was organized by VIT College Pune. This program was itselt a motivation to many of us. Many people who are zero level entrepreneur i.e., the people who have built up their business from the scratch have visited to VIT campus to encourage the students. These were people who had lot of experince and were successful in the diffrent fields. Some of them were from construction line, some were from film industry, some were from hotel industry, some were from IT background and some were from mechanical side which influenced us a lot. VIT conducts this program every year in the month of January. Many students attend this program every year with great enthusiasm
