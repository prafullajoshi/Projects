    About Prafulla Joshi



    Hi all, my name is Prafulla Joshi and I am a Trainee Software Engineer at Harbinger Systems Pvt Ltd since last 3 months. I love programming because it triggers my thought process. One of my favourite languages is Python. I am crazy at things I love. I am programming since last 7 years of my education and still doing with more and more passion. Other than programming I love to visit new places where I have not visited in lifetime before. It gives me energy to work more with enthusiasm. My simple funda about computer programming in to treat a computer as a human being. Computer is made by keeping human model in front of them and remember they are made to make our life simple and easy, not hard and difficult. Therefore if something is not working well or according to my expectation then only thing I tell to myself is Computer never makes mistakes, its a person sitting in front of it does. This philosophy helps me understand my mistakes very easily.
