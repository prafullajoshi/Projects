    Authorized Vehicle Access uing IoT

    With the help of Internet of Things, we are proposing a system where bike can be accessed with help of finger print scanning. In our system, a button will be replaced by a finger print sensor which will be used to authenticate the bike rider. Once the finger is matched with one in database then only the rider can ride that bike. An android application will be provided in this system. Whenever a finger is scanned then a notification will be sent to the owner of that bike. If the permission is granted by the owner then only his bike can be accessed for respective time decided by user. Owner can also track the current route of the bike.
